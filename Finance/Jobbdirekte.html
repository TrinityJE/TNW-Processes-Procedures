Jobbsys new procedure

Jobbsys is now created as a partner in Salesforce & each transaction should be recorded in there.

The reasons for this change are:

  • Having the values in SF will allow us to calculate our revenue more effeciently
  • We no longer need a special procedure for invoicing partners as POs will be directly included in monthly invoices
  • We will be able to match Jobbsys invoice against actual POs

CS procedure:

  • There is NO WAY to confirm the final EURO price before having an actual job listing
  • Once you receive the job listing from the selling partner, send it to Norway (Anne-Lene) to get a confirmation on the final price
  • Once you receive a confirmation from Jobbsys on the final price, you MUST confirm it back within 48h!!!
  • You will need to calculate the total net amount using this calculator, and then share this final price with the selling partner
  • At the same time, ask the selling partner to create a new customer in Salesforce if not created yet and to add Norway as Account-Country (no WW will be launched as it is NOT needed for the contract/PO creation)
  • Once the account exists, create a 12-month contract and a Contract-Product with the purchased services to Norway: standard without Finn, customized with Finn
  • Insert the the total net amount which you calculated using the calculator
  • Create a PO and POLI:
    • In case of Jobbsys/Purehelp, simply check if the "You can invoice us" amount corresponds to the amount Jobbsys asked for
    • In case of Jobbsys/Purehelp + Finn, change the "You can invoice us" amount with the amount Jobbsys asked for & the splits will adjust automatically

Finance procedure:

With Jobbsys in Salesforce, finance procedure becomes the same as for other partners:

  • Make sure the invoice includes EUR amount and that it matches with what's in "You can invoice us"
  • Process the invoice in Salesforce and add it to the file for our accountant
  • IN POs will be included in monthly invoices sent to partners
