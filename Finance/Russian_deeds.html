The procedure concerning deeds for our partner in Russia, HeadHunter, is the following:

  • At the beginning of each month Headhunter sends us Deeds (one for IN and one for OUT POs) together with their invoice
  • Values should match with reports "My IN POs" and "My OUT POs" (for OUT POs they have more flexibility which POs to invoice - conclusion is amount in the Deed should match with amount in the invoice they issue)
  • If all values are correct, approve the invoice in SF (find it, because most of times its not submitted) and confirm it to Melina via email
  • Finance responsible should sign, stamp and send scan of Deeds when possible
