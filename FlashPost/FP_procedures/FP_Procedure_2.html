FlashPost Errors and Rejected jobs

What is the procedure for errors in FlashPost?

The Network is responsible for treating all errors occurring in FlashPost.

The errors should be checked daily, preferably twice a day (in the morning and in the afternoon).

The procedure to follow is:

  • If you can treat the error alone, do so (temporary errors on the receiving partner side, etc.)
  • If you cannot treat the error alone, involve the selling / receiving partner (wrong credentials, no active contract, etc.)
    • Once the job is successfully posted, inform the partner
  • If you do not know the error, consult the rest of the team and / or Julien

What is the procedure for rejected jobs in FlashPost?

The Network is partly responsible for treating rejected jobs occurring in FlashPost. This responsibility is shared with the job distributors and the selling partners.

The rejected jobs should be checked daily (once a day is enough).

The procedure to follow is:

  • If the job was rejected because the customer is not allowed to post on a specific job board, the job distributor / partner should contact the customer and check with them; The Network will provide an assistance to partners during the adaption period (till end of August?)
  • If the job was rejected because the listing is a duplicate (only for create action), the job distributor / partner should contact the customer and check with them; The Network will provide an assistance to partners during the adaption period (till end of August?)
  • If the job was rejected because the user does not have permission to post on behalf of the customer specified, The Network should check this internally in the set-up and contact the partner in case more clarification is needed
