Edit WW functionality

This new functionality gives the selling parther the option to edit VALID WWs (non-expired and not linked to a contract).

To use it, the selling partner has to follow the below steps:

  • They should select all the WWs they want to edit and click on a new button 'Edit WW' that is displayed on the WW related list of the customer account
  • As mentioned above, only valid WWs will be submitted for editing - if a selected WW is not eligible for the edit action, they will receive an error (if this is the only WW he selected for the edit action), or a warning (in case there are several WWs selected and some of them are not eligible)
  • The selling partner can edit ALL fields in the 'Additional Information' section and they are obliged to summarize the changes in a new 'Edit Comment' field. All fields except 'Subsidiaries included' are mandatory ones

Edited WWs will have a new status 'To Edit' and will be resent to the receiving partners (they will see them in the WWs To Do and Edit table on their homepage). The due date of the WW will reset and they will have again 2 working days to answer them.

If the edited WW is not answered on time, they will be auto answered with their previous reply (so, if a WW was on 'Waiting for Approval' status, after the auto answer, it will be again in 'Waiting for Approval' status). Exception is the status 'To Edit' when a activity was marked, but the WW was NOT submitted for approval. In such a case, the receiving partner would have to either un tag the activity field if there was no WW product created and answer the WW with 'Done' or, if there was a WW product created, submit the WW for approval.
