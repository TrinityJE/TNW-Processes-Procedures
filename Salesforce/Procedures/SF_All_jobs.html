All Jobs

Whenever 'All Jobs' product is purchased, please follow this procedure:

  • CPQ: All jobs will be created as ad hoc listings with comment 'All jobs'
  • The unit should be set on 1, and the price should be the total price of the product.
  • Ideally, the selling partner should choose 'direct' as a posting method.

  • Salesforce: once the contract is won, and the CP is created, the 'All Jobs' product credits will be created as 'Job posting - Ad hoc' => here is where you need to go in, edit the CP and manually change the product into 'All Jobs'
  • Check if the posting method is 'Direct', and if not, change it accordingly
  • PO should be created manually by the admin

  • FlashPost: 'All Jobs' product should NOT be imported into FP (and it is not actually even possible to do so)
