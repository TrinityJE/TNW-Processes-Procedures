DE Internship credits

Here is the correct process when partner buys internship credits for StepStone.DE. It mainly involves manual amendment in Salesforce as per below:

  • CPQ: internship credits should be created as ad hoc listings (description local name: pro campus)
  • Salesforce: once the contract is won, and the CP is created, the internship credits will be created as 'Job posting - Ad hoc' for StepStone.DE => here is where you need to go in, edit the CP and manually change the product into 'Intership'
  • FlashPost: when importing the credits in FP, it will be displayed as 'Local Standard - Stepstone.de internship', and the channel will be StepStone.de internship standard
