How to treat ad-hoc products created in SF?

Whenever the selling partner creates a quote that contain 'ad -hoc' product, the system NO LONGER creates a PO for it automatically. (KM: I have doubt here - is it for all types, or only job listings?)

Admin must change the product into another SF product (standard, other, customized, alljobs, budget, etc.), in order to be able to import it, and for the PO to be created. (KM: I have doubt here again - shoudl we be changing ALL ad-hoc products, or just listing products?)

If you encounter a quote with 'ad-hoc' job listing product (KM: Again, - is it for all types, or only job listings?), follow this process:

  • Change the CP product into an existing one (check for comments in the quote, like alljobs, or contact the seller to get the correct information)
  • For non-listing products or listing products that are NOT importable into FP (like alljobs), you have to create the PO manually
  • For listing products that are importable into FP, depending on the posting method, you should:
    • In case of Self-Service, inform the selling partner that the change of the CP product is done and that they can import it to FP
    • In case of Regular FP, import the contract (or only the CP if upsell) in FP
    • In both cases, the PO will be created automatically upon the import to FP
