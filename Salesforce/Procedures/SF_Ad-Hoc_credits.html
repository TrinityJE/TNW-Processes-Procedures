How to treat ad-hoc products created in SF?

Whenever the selling partner creates a quote that contain 'ad -hoc' listing product, the system NO LONGER automatically creates a PO for it.

Admin should always change the product into another non-ad-hoc listing product (standard/customize/other job listing).

Why is this necessary? Because the listing products will ONLY be importable in FlashPost once the product is adjusted.

If you encounter a quote with 'ad-hoc' listing product, follow this procedure:

  • Change the CP product into an non-ad-hoc one (check for comments in the quote, or contact the seller to get the correct information)
  • For listing products that are NOT importable into FP (like Alljobs), create the PO manually
  • For listing products that are importable into FP, depending on the posting method, you should:
    • In case of Self-Service, inform the selling partner that the change of the CP product is done and that they can import it to FP
    • In case of Regular FP, import the contract (or only the CP if upsell) in FP
    • In both cases, the PO will be created automatically upon the import to FP

For non-listing ad-hoc products e.g. cvdb, communication, extra region, etc, the PO will be created automatically, and the CP created in Salesforce does NOT have the mention ad-hoc in the label.

NOTE: Sometimes partners make a mistake and create a non-listing ad-hoc product like extra region as a listing product, and the receiving partner approves the quote before we had a chance to revise. In that case, we should amend the CP and create the PO manually anyway.
