Mix & Match product


When Mix & Match product is created in Salesforce, a notification is sent to Slack channel #new-contracts.

Before validating the PO, you need to check the contract and all contract-products.

The procedure is as follows:

  • No protection should apply - WWs should be sent as decentralized and the system should ignore the value to protect (in case of legacy WW and value filled in, change it to 0,01€)
  • There must be min. 3 CPs, all Mix & Match (in case of a partner covering several countries, such as Tecoloco, as long as the countries are different, this is allowed)
  • Only local jobs (customized and standard) can be used
  • The discount can be max. 15% per CP, no agency commission allowed
  • The job listing must be identical for all receiving partners (if Self-Service, try to check that the job titles corresponds)
  • Translations allowed, of course
