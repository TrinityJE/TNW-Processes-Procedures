Conversion

Conversion is something we have been offering in these exceptional times as a way to compensate customers who were heavily impacted by Covid-19. These had the benefit of using-up the remaining inventory quickly so that customers didn’t end their contracts with a lot of unused credits. But now that we are reaching some stability, we would like to draw a clear set of rules for conversions of GFP products.

Conversions are given when a customer needs exceptional flexibility, they should not be taken for granted. All conversions of GFP products must be validated by The Network Sales team.

Conversions should only be requested under exceptional circumstances where there will be a striking underconsumption at the end of the contract. These will be studied on a case by case basis.

  • GFP credits may only be converted into local credits.
  • Conversions may only be requested during the last 3 months before the contract expires.
  • Conversions may only be done into our partner’s products. It is not acceptable to have GFP credits converted into products of niche job boards that are not offered via The Network.
