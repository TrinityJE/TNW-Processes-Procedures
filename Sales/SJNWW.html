Single job no WW

The Network allows partners to sell 1 listing per quarter and country without sending a WW. In these cases, the receiving partner cannot claim protection.

The conditions for selling a SJNWW are:

  • The listing must be sold at the full local rate card
  • There cannot be ANY discount
  • Agency Commission IS allowed
  • The customer is allowed to post using SJNWW in maximum 4 countries per quarter
  • The type of listings permitted are the basic standard and customized options (nothing extra such as refresh is allowed)

It is a concept created to serve small, immediate, one-off needs of a customer, considering these needs won’t jeopardize any local or international deal the partner might have.
