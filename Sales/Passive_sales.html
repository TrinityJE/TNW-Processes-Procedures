Passive Sales

In case you come across a company and identify they are not based in your country you are requested to send a lead to the partner covering the country where the Decision Maker is based.

If you are contacted by a customer based in a different country and you are certain that they only have local needs for your job board, you could perform a passive sale.

Based on Anti-trust Law in EU, partners do have the right to offer a local deal only, even prior to sending any Leads (and protection should be granted in this case if partner sent proposal + Lead). (we do not promote much it to our partners).

NOTE: No protection for ecommerce orders.
