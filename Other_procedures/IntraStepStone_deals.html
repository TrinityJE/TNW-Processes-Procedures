Intra-StepStone deals procedure

Whenever you treat a quote from one of the Intra-Stepstone countries (AT, BE, DE, LUX, NL) with a product going to another Intra-Stepstone country (AT, BE, DE, LUX, NL), check the following:

  • Comment in the quote that would indicate this is Intra-Stepstone and if this is a renewal or a new deal for them; check both the "Quote description" and "Internal Info/Remarks/Justification" sections
  • With/or without the comment being present, check our system and see if this is indeed the first time that we are recording such a transaction. IMPORTANT! This goes even for existing customers! It might be that there is international business recorded, but the Intra-Stepstone is not
  • In case this is either a new customer or such a business was never recorded in the past, follow this process:
    • In the CPQ it is stated that this is "renew" -> mark the CP as Intra-StepStone
    • In the CPQ it is states that this is "new" -> do NOT mark the CP as Intra-StepStone
    • In the CPQ there is no indication if this is "renew" or "new":
      • The CP is under 10K total net value -> mark the CP as Intra-StepStone
      • The CP is over 10K total net value -> check with SGO to get the information about "renew" or "new" and once you have this information, follow one of the steps above accordingly
