Nationwide listings UK

When a customer purchase nationwide listings for UK (Totaljobs), please follow this procedure:

  • Set up the customer as usual and sends the delegation
  • TJ reviews the contract, and in case it’s for nationwide listings, they will reply to the delegation saying that the channel should be changed to email override
  • Put the channel in email override and activate it
  • Once job is posted, it will be sent via email to TJ
  • TJ reviews the contract details, and in case it’s for nationwide listings, they will post it as such
  • In case of Self-Service and nationwide listings, there should be no issue as the jobs will be sent via email so TJ simply applies the previous step, checks the contract and if nationwide, posts it as such
