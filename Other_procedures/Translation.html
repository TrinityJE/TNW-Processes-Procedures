Translations

How to proceed when receiving translation request?

  • Send a new email to the translation agency and inlcude the following information:
    • Customer name
    • Original language, target language (i.e., from EN to DE)
    • Confirmation on SLA
    • Confirmatioin on price

  • REMEMBER! Never include the original email so they do not have direct contact information or the knowledge of our invoiced price

  • Once confrimed, get back to the selling partner with the SLA information.
  • In parallel, validate the translation PO in Salesforce (no WW for TNW needed)
  • Also, insert the necessary information into our translation tracking file so we can analyze requested translations over time
